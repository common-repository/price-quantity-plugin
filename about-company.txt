<br>
<br>
<li>1. Choose your desired Styles settings.
<br>
<li>2. Cut and paste sample short code into your posts or pages anywhere you wish for Price Quantity plugin to appear.
<br>
<li>3. Edit short code after you have pasted into posts or pages. example: [showQtyPriceTable value="0-25:$45,26-100:$30,100-200:$50" ] You might change to [showQtyPriceTable value="0-100:$65,26-100:$30,100-200:$50" ] Basically whatever your price quantity needs are you will set in the short code provided.
